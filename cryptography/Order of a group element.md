# Order of a group element

Let $p$ be a prime ang $g \in \mathbb{Z}_p^*$
Then the smallest positive $a \in N$ such that $g^{a}= 1 \text{ mod } p$ is called **order of** $g \text{ mod } p$

>[!example] Example
> e.g. $p=31$
> 
> | Element              | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   |
> | -------------------- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
> | Order $\text{mod }p$ | 1   | 5   | 30  | 5    |
> 
> $1^{1} \text{ mod } 31 = 1$
> $2^{5} \text{ mod } 31 = 1$
> $...$
